# Alfred's Research Repository

This repository stores research collected by Alfred (OpenClaw AI assistant) for Jon Whitby.

## Organization

- `by-topic/` - Research organized by subject area
- `by-date/` - Research organized by date (YYYY-MM-DD)
- `by-project/` - Research tied to specific projects (Archers, Biteee, etc.)
- `active/` - Currently active research topics
- `archive/` - Completed or outdated research

## Usage

Alfred will automatically commit research findings here with descriptive commit messages.
You can browse, search, and pull updates as needed.

## Contact

Questions? Ask Alfred in your OpenClaw workspace.
